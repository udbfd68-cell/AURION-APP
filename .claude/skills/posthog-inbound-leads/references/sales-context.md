# PostHog Sales Context

## Sales Assist Threshold

**$20,000 annual spend** is the minimum for sales-assisted process.

Below this threshold:
- Route to self-serve
- Be helpful via email with resources
- No calls unless extraordinary circumstances
- Direct to in-app support and community

## Qualification Criteria (from handbook)

When qualifying leads, consider:
- Products they want to use
- Company size and revenue
- Role of person who reached out
- Engineer involvement (critical!)

## Sales Process Overview

1. **People contact us** - via form or sales@posthog.com
2. **We assign and qualify** - determine if sales-assist appropriate
3. **First call** (30 min) - if qualified
4. **Second call** (60 min) - technical deep dive if needed
5. **Product evaluation** - trial with shared Slack
6. **Security & legal review** - if needed
7. **Commercial evaluation** - contract
8. **Closed - won or lost**

## When NOT to Offer Calls

From the handbook:

> "We often get requests for demos from leads or existing customers who are below our sales assist threshold, and who don't have a defined use case for PostHog. It usually comes in the form of 'show me all the features' or 'I need someone to demo to me.' These can be large time sinks because they are non-technical, don't have a clear idea of what they want, and are unlikely to ever grow into a sales-assist level customer."

> "If you realize that they will be too small (<$20k) to go through our sales-led process and you are unable to get this information from them, you should route to self-serve."

## Startup Plan

If eligible for startup plan, route to application form and disqualify (not immediate opportunity).

## Trial Durations (for qualified deals)

- $20-60K: 2 weeks
- $60K+: 4 weeks

## PostHog's Target Audience

Built for **product engineers** - technical folks. While non-technical people use PostHog successfully, the sales process assumes technical stakeholders are involved or will be.

## RFPs

- **Solicited RFPs** (with prior context): Generally reply
- **Unsolicited RFPs** (no context): Generally decline unless compelling
- Timebox effort on RFPs relative to opportunity size
